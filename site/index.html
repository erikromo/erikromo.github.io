About Me

During the eighth grade I decided it was time to purchase my very own laptop. But which laptop to buy ? That question led me to a rabbit hole of google searches where I ended up learning all about the basic components of computers.I learned about CPU’s ,GPU’s ,ram ,and the differences between SSD’s and the more traditional HDD’s .To this day I still enjoy reading about the breakthroughs in computer tech and the implications they might have in the future.Learning how to code was the next progression of my interest in technology .

.I enjoy programming because it feels like as if I were creating something from virtually nothing . Other than that I am quite a fan of a soccer .Seeing the steps companies like SpaceX are taking to make our society an interplanetary one .
