SalesForce Interviews

Over the course of my junior year I had the opportunity to interview several SalesForce employes . Here's what I learned.

The Interviews

What I Learned

Project Manager

A project manager leads a team in implementing new features of code . They accesses the scope of projects evaluate what needs to be done and organize that what has to be done. There work is essential has it helps to organize what needs to be done.A project manager breaks down complex tasks into small steps that are manageable .

Backend and Mid Tier Software Engineer

As a consumer you’ll never actually get to see what a back end engineer does . As they work behind the scenes making sure that the different components of a software project are talking to each other and the servers. Nevertheless , this work important as it enables complex software systems to function without a consumer having to interact the thousands of lines of codes that make up that system .

Front End Software Engineer

A front end engineer much like a UX designer plays an important role in what you see when use a piece of software .They are responsible for implementing the design created by a UX designer. As a result UX designers and Front End Software Engineer work closely together in order to devise and understand the scopes of different projects

UX Designer

Every seamless interaction that you’ve experienced while using a piece of software is a result of a UX designers work .The job of a UX designer encompasses many different facets . But the most important thing that a UX designers does is to create efficient design . In order to create efficient design a designer has to understand the needs of the client and the scope of the work .
